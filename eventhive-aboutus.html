Mission

EventHive aims to provide centralized web platform for discovering and managing events accessible to enhance engagement and organization's operation in the university.

Vision

To become the leading event management platform that creates a bright and warm space where no one will be left out.

Our Story

The journey to create EventHive embarked on September 2025. The idea sparked by the clutter of events swarming the social media because it can't guarantee full awareness of students which hinders participation. Information and updates regarding a particular events can come from multiple channels—showcasing creativity but lacking uniformity. EventHive streamlined the event system, enabling a user-friendly platform to make events recognized and every buzz count.
